Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is a comfort food eaten all around the world. It is fun and easy to make and can taste really good if you know what you're doing! Cook carefully.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Galic
  • Ketchup
  • Salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Get ground beef and throw in all the stuff
  2. Put it in the oven for 25 minutes
  3. Enjoy!
